What is a Website?

A website is a collection of web pages and related content that is identified by a common domain name and published on at least one web server.

Websites are typically dedicated to a particular topic or purpose, such as news, education, commerce, entertainment, or social networking.

The first website was created in 1991 by Tim Berners-Lee, and since then, the web has grown to over 1.8 billion websites today!

"The web does not just connect machines, it connects people." - Tim Berners-Lee
